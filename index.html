About Me

I am a self taught web developer currently living in Auckland, New Zealand.

My primary proficiency is in Ruby on Rails, which I began learning in 2013. I am competent in HTML, CSS, and frontend JavaScript (more details further down). In an effort to improve my skills, specifically in both frontend and backend JavaScript, I am currently working through Free Code Camp's curriculum.

I have a science degree in nutrition, and one of my main interests lies in health education, information and data. Consequently one of my goals is to utilize the web and associated technologies to deliver effective tools and materials relating to the health field.

However, I enjoy solving problems and building web apps in general, and am currently available for hire or freelance work of any description. Get in touch!
